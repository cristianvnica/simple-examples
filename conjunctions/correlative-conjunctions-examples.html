Correlative Conjunction Examples

Correlative conjunctions are pairs of conjunctions that work together in a sentence. They help to provide a balanced expression in English sentences.

Correlative Conjunctions

  • Either/Or: You can either stay here or come with us.
  • Neither/Nor: He is neither smart nor diligent.
  • Both/And: She is both intelligent and hardworking.
  • Not only/But also: He not only studies hard, but also excels in sports.
  • Whether/Or: I don’t know whether she will come or not.
