Conjunctive Adverbs Examples

Conjunctive adverbs are a part of speech in English and many other languages. They are used to connect independent clauses, adding more flow to the writing.

Conjunctive Adverbs

  • However: I wanted to eat out. However, I didn't have enough money.
  • Therefore: It was raining. Therefore, I decided to stay at home.
  • Furthermore: He is a great team player. Furthermore, he has exceptional leadership skills.
  • Consequently: I didn't study for the test. Consequently, I failed.
  • Meanwhile: I started cooking dinner. Meanwhile, she set the table.
  • Otherwise: Please hurry up. Otherwise, we will be late.
  • Nevertheless: I knew it was expensive. Nevertheless, I decided to buy it.
